Why Should We care?

  • 70% of Earth's oxygen comes from ocean.
  • Over 8 million tons of plastic center the ocean each year.
  • Marine species are choking on plastic waste and dying from oil pills.

Our Mission: A Cleaner Ocean,A better Future..

ClearWave's core initiatives:

Beach Cleanup Warriors

Removing plastic waste from coastal areas

Sustainable Fishing Champions

Training fishermen to use eco-friendly nets and reduce by-catch.

Zero Plastic Movement

Promoting reusable products and recycling.
